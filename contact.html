Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How secure is MyApp?
We use BCrypt encryption for passwords and JWT tokens for authentication. Your data is protected at every layer with enterprise-grade security.
Is MyApp free to use?
Yes! MyApp offers a free tier with all core features. Premium plans are available for power users who need advanced capabilities.
How do I reset my password?
Visit the login page and click "Reset Password". Enter your email and we'll send you a secure reset code instantly.
What technology powers MyApp?
Our backend is built on Spring Boot with MySQL database. The frontend uses Next.js, deployed on Vercel with the API on Railway.
